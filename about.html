OUR STORY

We raise, buy, polish, and resell horses, and also take consignments. We offer quality weanlings, yearlings, brood mares, & trail horses. Starting in 1983 and going full-time in 1992, we have sold horses all through the nation. We strive to have the best treatment for our animals.
